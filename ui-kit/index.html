THE TOMOCHELLA SPIRIT

A new generation festival, TOMOCHELLA has been inviting since 2016 in the Beaches of Barcelona, wild and exciting artists who draw the music of our time, and who attract up to 80,000 people. An electrical programming that crosses the bridges between indie, electro and urban.

A FESTIVAL WITH A CAUSE

Everything started from the desire to produce a festival rooted in current issues, TOMOCHELLA is a celebration whose objective is to raise awareness, influence audiences, change behaviour and participate in a grassroots movement in which every citizen can participate.
